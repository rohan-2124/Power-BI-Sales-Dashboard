# 📊 Power BI Sales Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an interactive Sales Dashboard built using Power BI. It helps analyze sales performance, profit, customer contribution, product performance, and regional sales trends through easy-to-understand visualizations.

The dashboard is designed to provide quick business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Dashboard Purpose

The purpose of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor overall sales performance
* Track profit and quantity sold
* Identify top-performing customers
* Analyze product category performance
* Compare sales across different states
* Understand customer payment preferences
* Support business decisions with visual insights

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales Amount
* Total Profit
* Total Quantity Sold

---

## 📊 Visualizations Used

### KPI Cards

Displays:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Quantity

### Profit by Sub-Category (Bar Chart)

Shows which product sub-categories generate the highest profit.

### Amount by Month (Column Chart)

Displays monthly sales trends and helps identify seasonal patterns.

### Amount by State (Bar Chart)

Compares sales performance across different states.

### Amount by Customer Name (Bar Chart)

Highlights top customers based on sales amount.

### Quantity by Payment Mode (Donut Chart)

Shows how customers prefer to make payments.

### Quantity by Category (Donut Chart)

Displays quantity distribution across product categories.

### Interactive Slicers

Allows users to filter dashboard data by:

* Quarter
* Other available selections
